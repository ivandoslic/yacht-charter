# Dolce Vita

## Overview

<--!StartFragment-->

Fascinating gulet Dolce Vita is the most luxurious gulet in Croatia. She boats with a 34 m in length and very wide beam of 7,5 m, which is a guarantee of comfort and spacious accommodation.

Dolce Vita is widely known for her extraordinary luxury and superb on board service, the gulet was cruising in Turkey and in 2015 she has finally started her cruises in Croatia.

Gulet Dolce Vita has 5 premium cabins to accommodate 10 guests.
