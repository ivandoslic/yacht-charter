# Simull

## Overview

<!--StartFragment-->

Motor yacht Simull with the Sport Flybridhe and timeless design features a superb yach for your holiday. Doubtedly,dynamic and chic silhouette,generous living space and three elegant en-suite cabins as her basic features make this Prestige yacht magnificient.Doubtlessly,with the speed of 24 knots and a small draft, Simull will discover you the most fascinating hidden gems of the Adriatic coastline. With all this in mind, we can declare Simull as one of the most desirable smaller yachts in Croatia.

*GUEST ACCOMMODATION*

Motor yacht Simull can accommodate 6 guests and 2 crew members.All three guests cabins are on the lower deck,with private head and shower compartments. As a matter of fact, the Master full-beam cabin is accessed via its own stairway from the salon.Furthermore,the forepeak Vip cabin has a double bed. Finally,the third cabin has two single beds that can slide together to make a double.

*OUTSTANDING FEATURES*

Simull inherits the finest attributes of the Prestige yachts.Basically,this Prestige 630 SportYacht features a refined, luxurious interior and impeccable finish. Flybridge is composed of a full helm station and a U-shaped salon,which transforms into a sundeck and a grill. Besides,sunbathing area on the foredeck is superb for taking sun and beautiful views of the surroundings. Al-fresco dining area on the aft is another charming part of this 20-meter-long Prestige yacht. Simull's modern interior is furnished in harmony. Likewise,the cut-down shape windows allow natural light to the interior and offer an incomparable 360 view from the salon. During the night,when illuminated by underwater lights,Simull shines in its particular splendor.This excellent Prestige yacht awaits for you to explore the Adriatic's secluded bays and coves! Simull Yacht for Charter is availible in Croatia and is located in Trogir as the Homeport.
