# ACAPELLA

## Overview

<!--StartFragment-->

Acapella is the pride and joy of a very experienced owner who has already established an excellent reputation in the Croatian charter market. She is finished to the highest standards, provides great amenities, and has a superyacht crew offering unparalleled service. Her Captain’s knowledge is extensive and her chef is simply superb. \
\
**KEY FEATURES**

*High Standard Accommodations:* Three full-beam Master cabins and two triple cabins designed for utmost comfort, featuring king-sized beds, wall-mounted TVs, and spacious en-suite bathrooms with walk-in showers.

*Elegant Main Salon:* Relaxing ambiance in the main salon with harmonious colors and panoramic windows, bathing the space in natural light.

*Alfresco Dining:* A spacious aft deck with a dining table for delightful alfresco dining experiences.

*Leisure Spaces:* Ample leisure areas, including a wide deck with a jacuzzi, provide inviting spaces for relaxation and enjoyment while onboard.

*Dedicated Crew:* A committed team of 9 ensuring impeccable service and a memorable stay for all guests.
