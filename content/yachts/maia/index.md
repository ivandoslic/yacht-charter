# MAIA

## Overview

<!--StartFragment-->

Launching in 2025, MAIA is the embodiment of Captain Filip Jakir's maritime expertise and passion for sailing. This exquisite yacht seamlessly blends classic motorboat elegance with modern innovation and luxury. Designed for 12 guests in 6 meticulously crafted cabins, MAIA offers ultimate comfort and sophistication. More than just a beautiful vessel, MAIA is a sanctuary of relaxation and adventure. With Filip Jakir's renowned commitment to exceptional service, guests can expect an unforgettable holiday experience where timeless design meets modern elegance.

*Visionary Design:* MAIA is the latest visionary creation by Captain Filip Jakir, set to launch in 2025, and represents the culmination of Filip's extensive maritime experience and passion for sailing.

*Luxurious Guest Accommodation:* 6 beautifully crafted cabins accommodating 12 guests, with each cabin carefully designed to provide the utmost comfort and sophistication.

*Balance of Relaxation and Adventure:* Offering both a sanctuary for relaxation and an opportunity for thrilling adventures, MAIA offers the exceptional standards Filip Jakir is renowned for, ensuring unforgettable holidays.

*Classic Design Meets Modern Elegance:* With every detail thoughtfully considered, MAIA effortlessly combines classic motorboat aesthetics with modern touches, resulting in a luxurious experience that embraces both tradition and contemporary style.

*Exceptional Fuel Efficiency:* MAIA consumes just 35-40 liters per hour, thanks to her optimized hull, engine, and propeller design. This efficiency reduces APA requirements by 25%, delivering a more sustainable and cost-effective yachting experience.
